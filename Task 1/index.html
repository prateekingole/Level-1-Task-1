About

P-tech is a donor-supported tax-exempt 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization

Our mission: to help people learn to code for free. We accomplish this by creating thousands of videos, articles, and interactive coding lessons - all freely available to the public. We also have thousands of P-tech study groups around the world.

Donations to P-tech go toward our education initiatives, and help pay for servers, services, and staff.
